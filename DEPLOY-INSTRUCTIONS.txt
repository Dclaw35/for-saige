HOW TO UPLOAD TO GITHUB PAGES

1. Unzip this folder.
2. Open your `for-saige` repository on GitHub.
3. Click `Add file` then `Upload files`.
4. Drag in:
   - index.html
   - style.css
   - script.js
   - the images folder
5. Commit changes.
6. Go to Settings > Pages.
7. Under `Build and deployment`, choose:
   - Source: Deploy from a branch
   - Branch: main
   - Folder: /root
8. Save.
9. Wait about 1 to 3 minutes.
10. Your site will be at:
   https://YOUR-GITHUB-USERNAME.github.io/for-saige/

If you see a 404, wait a couple minutes and refresh.
